Vision & Mission

Vision

"To be a premier department in Data Science education, nurturing data-literate professionals who drive innovation through evidence-based solutions and cutting-edge analytics."

Mission

1 To impart quality education in Data Science with a strong foundation in mathematics, statistics, and computer science.
2 To develop skilled professionals capable of extracting actionable insights from large-scale datasets using modern tools.
3 To promote research in data analytics, AI, and interdisciplinary applications through industry partnerships.
4 To nurture ethical data practitioners with strong problem-solving skills and social responsibility.

Core Focus Areas

Big Data Technologies

Hadoop, Spark, and distributed computing.

Statistical Modeling

Advanced statistical analysis and prediction.

Machine Learning

Supervised and unsupervised learning algorithms.

Data Visualization

Tableau, Power BI, and visual analytics.

Department Facilities

Data Analytics Lab

Equipped with high-performance computing systems, Hadoop clusters, and tools like Python, R, Tableau, and Apache Spark for large-scale data processing.

ML Research Center

Dedicated space with GPU-accelerated workstations for training machine learning models, deep learning projects, and NLP research.

Digital Library Access

Access to IEEE, ACM, and Springer journals with focus areas in data mining, analytics, and computational statistics research.
