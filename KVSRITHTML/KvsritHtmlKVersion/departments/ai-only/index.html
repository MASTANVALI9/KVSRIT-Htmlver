Vision & Mission

Vision

"To emerge as a nationally recognized department in Artificial Intelligence, producing innovative thinkers who design intelligent systems that positively impact society."

Mission

1 To provide a comprehensive curriculum in Artificial Intelligence covering foundational theory, algorithms, and practical applications.
2 To develop skilled engineers capable of designing, implementing, and deploying intelligent systems across diverse domains.
3 To foster research in emerging AI areas such as generative AI, reinforcement learning, and autonomous systems.
4 To build strong industry connections for internships, projects, and placements in AI-driven organizations.

Department Facilities

AI Computing Lab

State-of-the-art lab with NVIDIA GPUs, TensorFlow and PyTorch workstations for training deep neural networks and deploying AI models.

Robotics Lab

Hands-on robotics and IoT prototyping facility with Arduino, Raspberry Pi, robotic arms, and sensor kits for building autonomous systems.

Research Library

Curated collection of AI research papers, textbooks, and access to IEEE, ACM, and arXiv for staying current with AI advances.
