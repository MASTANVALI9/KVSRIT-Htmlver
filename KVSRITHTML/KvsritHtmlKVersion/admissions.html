Process

How to Apply

The admission process at KVSRIT follows a systematic approach. Please follow these steps carefully to ensure a smooth admission experience:

1

Verify Eligibility Criteria

Carefully review and confirm that you meet the eligibility requirements for your desired program (B.Tech, M.Tech, MBA, MCA, or Diploma). Ensure you satisfy the minimum academic qualifications and entrance examination prerequisites as specified by APSCHE.

2

Register & Appear for Entrance Examination

Complete your registration and appear for the appropriate entrance examination as mandated for your chosen program. This includes AP EAMCET, ECET, ICET, PGECET, GATE, or POLYCET, depending on the course requirements.

3

Participate in Counseling or Apply Directly

Category A (70% - Convener Quota): Participate in the centralized online counseling process conducted through the official APSCHE portal. Category B (30% - Management Quota): Submit your application directly to the college administration.

4

Document Verification & Submission

Present all mandatory documents for verification, including your entrance examination rank card, original academic certificates (SSC & Intermediate), transfer certificate, recent passport-size photographs, and caste certificate (if applicable). Ensure all documents are authentic and complete.

5

Fee Payment & Admission Confirmation

Complete the prescribed fee payment as per the institutional fee structure. Upon successful payment and completion of all formalities, you will receive your official admission confirmation letter. Welcome to KVSRIT â€“ your journey towards engineering excellence begins here.

Categories

Admission Categories

In accordance with APSCHE regulations, KVSRIT offers admissions through two distinct categories. Please identify and proceed with the category applicable to your situation:

Convener Quota

Category A (70%)

Centralized Counseling Process

Seats under this category are allocated through the centralized online counseling process conducted by APSCHE, based on the merit rank secured in AP EAMCET, ECET, ICET, or PGECET examinations. Seat allotment is conducted transparently through the official APSCHE counseling portal.

Visit APSCHE Portal
Management Quota

Category B (30%)

Direct Admission Process

Seats under this category are filled directly by the college management on a merit basis, in strict adherence to APSCHE guidelines and regulations. Eligible candidates may apply directly to the institution for admission consideration under this quota.
